    About

    The Anointed One

    The Allanridge born Deejay and producer's skills and hunger to become better every time has resulted in his rise to fame and now recognised outside the boarders of Free State. This journey also came with a few accomplishments.

    A list that includes being named as an Ambassador of an energy drink "Zzero Hangover" & sparkling wine “Lavante”, interviews on national radio stations and the entertainment show "Real Goboza", profile publications on various newspaper and magazines like Daily Sun, Vista newspaper and Enthooze magazine, has his own complete and up & running website (www.fantasthedj.co.za) to name a few.

    His charity drive includes a soccer tournament and an invitation of beneficiary schools based in Allanridge. The charity drive hands over food parcels and school shoes to the under privileged children. The day of the drive is filled with joyous celebrations with sports and music and a few motivational words from high figures from the Matjhabeng Municipality and now the MEC of Arts & Culture who has now adopted the charity drive as one of the events she attends annually.
